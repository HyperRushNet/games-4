1010 Color Match
Potato Jam

About

1010 Color Match is a unique puzzle game where you need to drag and drop different pieces of color plates to match the colors together and eliminate the color plates! If you cannot place any more color plates on the big board, you lose. Try to think of creative ways to match the colors to get the most points and brain exercise! Even better, this game auto-saves so you can pick up the matching anytime!
