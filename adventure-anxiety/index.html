Adventure with Anxiety
Nicky Case

About

In this fun human psychology simulation game, you are your own worst fear: anxiety. Your job is to tell the human what to do so that the human is safe from dangers, aka freak them out as much as you can so they wouldn't be in danger. In each scenario, click on one of the three options to decrease the human's energy level, and when the energy level is empty, you beat the level! There are many different levels for you to explore, so have fun scaring the human!
