Ball Sort Puzzle

About

In this interesting modern puzzle game Ball Sort Puzzle, your goal is to click on a ball at the top of any tube, and click on another tube with the same type of ball at the top of it to drop it. By rearraging the balls, you can sort each type of balls into their own seperate tube and advance to the next, harder level. After each level you will earn points which you can use to purchase different styles of tubes. This game not only exercises your brain, it also keeps you entertained! Good luck sorting these carefully mixed up balls!
